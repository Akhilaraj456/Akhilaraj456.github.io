Lethal Dose

An interpretable machine learning pipeline that predicts LD50 drug toxicity directly from molecular structure. Molecules were encoded with Morgan fingerprints and physicochemical descriptors, then modelled with Ridge Regression, Random Forest, and XGBoost, reaching an R² of 0.65 in line with published benchmarks. A weighted ensemble reduced variance, and SHAP attribution powered a "Toxic Substructure Hunter" that localises the high-risk fragments of a molecule. In drug discovery, a prediction is only useful if a chemist can see why it was made.

Python · RDKit · XGBoost · scikit-learn · SHAP · PyTDC · NumPy · Pandas · Matplotlib
